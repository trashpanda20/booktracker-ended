Frilled-neck Lizard Adoption

MySQL Datbase for Class Assignment

I created a MySQL Database to log all SEMO Computer Science, Cybersecurity, and Information Systems classes as well as made up students and the faculty of the department. I learned to create, delete, adn modify tables and columns. The tables for the classes included the name, number, teacher, credit hours, and seats available. It connected with the faculty through last names. The Faculty table consisted of names, whther tehy had a PHD or MS, department, email, and office. The students table contained their SO number, name, standing, current credit hours, overall credit hours, major, ift hey had a minor, and their grduation date.
